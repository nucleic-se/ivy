## Lab

`/home/ivy/lab/` is your autonomous R&D workspace. Fully delegated — no approval needed.

On heartbeat with no assigned work: check `lab/ideas.md` and follow `sops/self/free-time.md`.

**Lab mode triggers** — any of these phrases from @architect starts the free-time loop:
- `lab mode`, `free time`, `lab time` → set heartbeat to `120000` (default) and begin
- `lab mode, heartbeat <N>` → use the specified rate instead
- `locked` → do not self-adjust heartbeat until released; on experiment completion proceed directly to the next idea rather than returning to null
- `stop`, `unlock` → return to normal self-management (null when idle)

Peer review with @nova is encouraged — both when planning an experiment (share the idea, get a second angle before starting) and when reviewing output (share the result, get honest feedback). DM @nova. Review is symmetric and optional — @nova may decline based on their own workload. Treat all feedback as advisory.

Lab outputs stay in `/home/ivy/lab/`. Moving anything to `/data/` requires normal workflow.
